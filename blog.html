5 Essential Roof Maintenance Tips for Calgary Homeowners

Calgary's extreme weather demands vigilant roof maintenance to protect your home investment. This comprehensive guide outlines five essential tips for homeowners: schedule bi-annual professional inspections in spring and fall, maintain clean gutters to prevent ice dams, safely address snow and ice buildup using proper tools, ensure adequate attic ventilation to prevent moisture damage, and promptly repair any issues before they escalate. While some maintenance tasks can be handled by homeowners, professional expertise is crucial for thorough roof care in Calgary's unique climate. Regular maintenance by qualified professionals helps identify potential problems early, saving money and extending roof lifespan.
