Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical roof installation take?

Most residential roofing projects are completed within 2-3 days, weather permitting. Larger or more complex projects may take 4-5 days. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

What warranties do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive 25-year warranty on materials and a 10-year warranty on workmanship. Our warranties are fully transferable and cover both materials and labor, ensuring your investment is protected for decades to come.

How do you handle unexpected issues during installation?

Our experienced team conducts thorough pre-installation inspections to identify potential issues. If unexpected problems arise, we immediately communicate with you, provide detailed documentation, and present cost-effective solutions.

What types of roofing materials do you work with?

We specialize in all major roofing materials including asphalt shingles, metal roofing, slate, and synthetic materials. Our team can help you select the best option based on your budget, style preferences, and climate considerations.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with several financial institutions to offer flexible financing options with competitive rates. We can help you explore payment plans that fit your budget while getting the quality roof you need.
